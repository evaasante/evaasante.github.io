After the events of Captain America: Civil War, T'challa returns home to the strikingly, reclusive African nation of Wakanda to take his rightful place as king. However, a mighty enemy suddenly reappears challenging T'challa for the throne. T'challa's bravery as king and as Black Panther, gets tested when he's drawn into a battle that puts the fate of Wakanda and the entire world at risk. confronted with disloyalty and danger, the young king must assemble his allies and release the full power of Black Panther to defeat his enemies and ensure the safety of his people.
