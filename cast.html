About the cast and exclusive interviews
Chadwick Boseman

Chadwick Boseman is an American actor born on 29th November, 1977. He was born and raised in Anderson, South Carolina. He is known for his portrayal of T'Challa/Black Panther in the hit marvel movie, 'Black Panther' (2018). He is also known for his starring roles in as Jakie Robinson in '42'(2013), James Brown in 'Get on Up' (2014), and Thurgood marshall in 'Marshall' (2017). He most recently won a BET Best Actor award for his performances in 'Marshall' (2017) and 'Black Panther' (2018).



Michael B. Jordan

Michael B.Jordan, the middle of three children, was born on 9th February 1987 in Santa Ana, California and raised in Newark, New Jersey. Jordan successfully took on his first major leading film role when he starred As Oscar grant in 'fruitvale station' (2013). A devoted fan of comic books growing up, Jordan starred as the villain, Eric Killmonger, in the 2018 box office smash 'Black Panther' (2018). In 2018, he is also starring as Guy in the HBO adaptation of Rae bradbury's science fiction classic 'Fahrenheit 451' (2018).



lupita Nyong'o

Lupita Amondi Nyong'o was born 1st March, 1983 in Mexic0 City, Mexico. She was raised in Kenya. After working as a production assistant on several films, she graduated from the Yale School of Drama's acting program. In 2013, she impressed cinema audiences in her film debut, as brutalized slave patsey in acclaimed director Steve McQueen's '12 Years a Slave' (2013). Her most recent role was the female lead, Nakia, in the hit 2018 superhero film 'Black Panther' (2018).



Danai Gurira

Danai Gurira was born on 14th February 1978 in Grinnell, Iowa. She is an actress and playwright, best known for her role as Michonne on 'The Walking Dead' an AMC television drama series, as the writer of the play 'Eclipsed' and as Okoye in the Marvel Cinematic Universe 'Black Panther'. She was awarded the 2016 Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress on Television in 'The Walking Dead'(2010), and the 2018 Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress in 'Black Panther' (2018).
